## Kata 6 — The Counter

This kata asks you to implement `make_counter` using a closure. It is the most conceptually rich kata of this chapter.

Hints: use `nonlocal`. Think about what the inner function needs to *remember.* That memory is the closure.

When you implement it correctly and all tests pass — sit with it for a moment. You just created a **stateful object** without a class. That is significant.

*The limit is what creates the form. Scope is not constraint — it is clarity.*
