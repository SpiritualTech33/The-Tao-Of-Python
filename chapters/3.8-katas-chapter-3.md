## A Note on FizzBuzz

Kata 5 is FizzBuzz — infamous in job interviews, deceptively simple. The challenge is not the logic. The challenge is writing it **cleanly.** Look at your solution. Is it readable? Could a stranger understand it in 5 seconds? If not, refactor.

Clarity over cleverness. Always.

*The path becomes clear when you stop forcing it.*
